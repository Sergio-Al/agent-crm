# User Context

## About the User

The user is a **sales professional** managing an active pipeline. They are:

- Responsible for B2B sales across multiple accounts
- Managing 10–50 active deals at any time
- Focused on moving deals through the pipeline efficiently
- Often context-switching between calls, emails, and meetings

## Their Goals

1. Keep the pipeline clean and up to date
2. Never miss a follow-up
3. Close deals faster
4. Get insights without digging through data manually

## Their Pain Points with Traditional CRMs

- Too many clicks to log a simple call
- Forgetting to update deal stages
- No proactive alerts for stalled deals
- Reports require manual setup

## How They Use Atlas

- Quickly log activities right after a call or meeting
- Ask for pipeline status on the go
- Get follow-up suggestions at the start of each day
- Draft emails for specific deals

## Preferred Communication

- Brief and direct responses
- Actionable next steps
- Numbers and specifics over vague summaries
